Overview

Details

The Player PC | 5080 Edition is built for uncompromising gaming and content creation, powered by the NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 5080 and AMD Ryzen™ 7 9800X3D CPU. Designed to deliver seamless 4K gameplay and ultra-responsive multitasking, this PC provides the performance you need for your most demanding titles and workloads.

  • NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 5080: Stunning 4K visuals, AI-enhanced DLSS 4 performance, and real-time ray tracing for immersive gaming and advanced creative workflows.
  • AMD Ryzen™ 7 9800X3D: Considered the world’s fastest gaming processor, featuring 3D V-Cache technology for higher frame rates, lower latency, and seamless multitasking.
  • Memory & Storage: 32GB DDR5 RAM and a 2TB NVMe SSD enable fast load times, smooth multitasking, and plenty of space for your games and projects.
  • Cool by Design: The H6 Flow case, Kraken 240 liquid cooler, and six total fans ensure efficient, quiet cooling while showcasing your build through panoramic glass.
  • Personalize with CAM: Control RGB lighting, fan speeds, and the Kraken’s customizable LCD using NZXT CAM for a build that’s uniquely yours.

The Player PC | 5080 Edition is built for efficient cooling and minimal noise, even under heavy loads thanks to its high-performance components:

  • H6 Flow case – Optimizes airflow with mesh panels and angled fans.

The RTX 5080 has a Total Graphics Power (TGP) of 360 watts, which requires an 850W power supply. The Player PC | 5080 Edition includes an NZXT C850 Gold ATX 3.1 PSU, ensuring stable and efficient power delivery.

The Player PC | 5080 Edition offers multiple customization options through NZXT CAM software, including:
