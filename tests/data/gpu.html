      from completed build DeepCool CH160 - First Build Ever

      I chose this GPU for my first build, specifically to handle photo editing in Lightroom and Photoshop, as well as some 4K video editing. So far, it’s been a solid performer across the board. With 12GB of GDDR6 VRAM, this GPU offers plenty of memory for handling high-resolution images and complex editing tasks without slowing down. In Lightroom and Photoshop, everything runs smoothly—even when working with large files and multiple layers. The GPU acceleration in these programs really benefits from the RTX 3060’s power, making the editing process faster and more efficient. The dual-fan design of the Ventus 2X keeps the card cool under load, and I haven’t noticed any significant noise during operation. It’s a great option if you’re looking for a well-rounded and budget-friendly GPU that can handle creative tasks like photo and video editing without breaking the bank.
